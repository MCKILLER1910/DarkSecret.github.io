The Amargeddon


A demon in disguised, was once sealed up by humanity for eternity until recently awakened by a person who desired for power to protect his loved one. The demon granted him the power but also turned him into an amalgamation of nightmares, causing mayhem where he was locked up. Now once again roaming in the mortal world, the Armageddon will wander around, filling his hunger for blood after decades of sleep. If you hear on the streets some weird scratching and hissing, you better run.
