ABOUT US


Hello, we are the RPB, group of artist and writer who leading this project. We like writing up dark fantasy and sci-fi story and rp in the world we created. This is a world where among us human, there those who take form as animatronic, or in other words, animal robots. Each of them have their own story and goal in life. We want to showcase a little bit about our story and our gallery hope you guys enjoying reading and seeing them.

The character here are owned by me. Please dont steal or repost without permission. The design are inspired from Five Nights at Freddy's franchise by the one and only Scott Cawthon.
